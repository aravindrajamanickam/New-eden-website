Government Schemes

Schemes Assits
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) 1. Insurance protection for food crops, oilseeds and annual horticultural/ commercial crops notified by state government.

2. Uniform maximum premium for all farmers : i) Kharif season - 2% of sum insured. ii) Rabi Season 1.5% of sum insured. iii) Annual commercial/horticultural crops - 5% of sum insured.

3. The difference between actual premium and the rate of Insurance payable by farmers shall be shared equally by the Centre and State.

4. Claims of full Sum Insured (SI), without capping or reduction in SI.

5. If the sowing is not done due to adverse weather/climate, claims upto 25% of sum insured will be paid for prevented sowing/planting risk.

6. When the crop yield is less than the guaranteed yield of notified crops, the claim payment equal to shortfall in yield is payable to all insured farmers
Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS) 1. Insurance protection for notified food crops, oilseeds and annual horticultural/commercial crops

2. Uniform maximum premium for all farmers like PMFBY : a) Kharif season - 2% of sum insured. b) Rabi Season 1.5% of sum insured. c) Commercial/horticultural crops 5% of sum insured

3.The difference between actual premium and the rate of Insurance payable by farmers shall be shared equally by the Centre and State

4.When the weather indices (rainfall/ temperature/relative humidity/wind speed etc.) is different (less or higher) from the Guaranteed Weather Index of notified crops, the claim payment equal to deviation/shortfall is payable to all insured farmers of notified area

Coconut Palm Insurance Scheme (CPIS) 1. Insurance protection for Coconut Palm growers.

2 Premium rate per palm ranges from Rs. 9.00 (in the plant age group of 4 to 15 years) to Rs. 14.00 (in the plant age group of 16-60 years).

3. 50-75% subsidy of premium is provided to all types of farmers.

4. When the palm damaged, the claim payment equal to input cost loss damage is payable to the insured in notified area
Unified Package Insurance Scheme (UPIS) as pilot in notified districts. 1.Insurance Scheme (UPIS) as pilot in notified districts.

2. To provide financial protection & comprehensive risk coverage of crops, assets, life, and student safety to farmers.

3. Pilot will include seven section Viz., crop Insurance (PMFBY/WBCIS), Loss of Life (PMJJBY), Accidental Death & Disability (PMSBY), Student Safety, Household, Agriculture implements & Tractor.

4. Crop Insurance will be compulsory. However, farmers can choose at least two section from remaining.


5. Farmers may be able to get all requisite insurance products for farmers through one simple proposal/ application Form and through single window.

6. Two flagship schemes of the Government viz PMSBY & PMJJBY have been included apart from insurance of assets. y Pilot scheme will be implemented through single window.

7. Processing of claims (other than Crop Insurance) on the basis of individual claim report.
